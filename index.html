Global Education Observatory

A unified initiative integrating open education data, geospatial analytics, and AI for global insight, monitoring, & policy advancement.

Open Education Data Commons (OEDC)

The world's first harmonized open school-level education dataset — infrastructure, personnel, resources, outcomes in LMICs.

GEDEx

Regional EMIS expansion in Southeast Asia — AI-aligned data systems for schools, learning outcomes, equity, digital visibility.

torchSDG

AI-powered mapping of SDG education indicators — school-level prediction, geospatial modeling, insight into access & learning.
